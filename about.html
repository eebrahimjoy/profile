I was born in Mymensingh, Bangladesh. My childhood memories are filled with delightful moments spent with my parents in Mymensingh, where I received my primary education.

Later, I moved to Sreepur, Gazipur, for my secondary education, which I recall as the most memorable time of my life. I successfully passed my Secondary School Certificate with a fantastic grade of 4.56 out of 5.00, majoring in Science, from Treemohoni School.

After that, I moved to Dhaka and enrolled at Holy Child College in Uttara, where I lived in a hostel and pursued my passion for science. In 2014, I completed my Higher Secondary Certificate with flying colors, scoring a perfect grade of 5.00 out of 5.00

As technology has always fascinated me, I decided to pursue a degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Daffodil International University. During my academic journey, I participated in numerous math olympiads, programming contests, and annual tours with my friends and soulmates to places like Cox's Bazar, Saint Martin, and Kuakata. However, just before completing my graduation in 2018, I lost my father on November 17, 2018, at Mymensing Medical Hospital. Despite this setback, I completed my graduation with a commendable CGPA of 3.79 out of 4.00 in January 2019.

After finishing my studies, I gained experience working in various organizations, which I have detailed in my experience section. I got married on September 17, 2021, and in February 2023, I moved to Dubai, UAE, for job purposes. Currently, I reside in International City, Dubai, UAE. I am grateful for my life journey, which has taught me the value of hard work, dedication, and the joy of learning and growing in the field of technology.
